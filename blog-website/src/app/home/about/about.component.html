About me

Ever since I was a little kid I was interesting in everything related to computers.

I started my professional carreer as an Accountant. Working with numbers seems to be okay for me.

After working in the field for about 1 year, I quickly realised that this was not what I wanted to do for my entire life.

At that moment I started thinking, what am I enjoying most in life. And that's the moment I dropped out of school and signed up for an education in IT.

Three years later I set up Gryphonheart Studios together with 4 of my class mates. We were going to make games together because we were very passionate about games at that point.

Unfortunately, after 1,5 years of hard word on our new game, we came to the hard realisation that creating games is a very costly affair and the future for Gryphonheart did not look that bright. Thus after mutual understanding we all went our ways and decided to go into Software Engineering instead.

There I found the complexity of creating tailor-made solutions for customers with very specific needs. This was challenging in its own way, which made me want to explore more of the industry.

Now I'm working for Info Support where I am a Medior Software Consultant. Aside from being a software consultant, I can be found in our knowledge center where I teach new and upcoming IT professionals in .NET and Angular.
